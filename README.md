## 🎓 Vellor 👉 [Repository Link](https://github.com/dhaatrik/vellor)

<div align="center">
  <img src="./public/vellor_logo.png" width="100"/>
</div>

#### **The Mission Statement:** 
Empowering independent educators with a completely free, open-source "Operating System" that makes managing a tutoring business effortless while keeping student data 100% private and offline.

#### **Current Progress:** 
Vellor has evolved into its **v4.0.0 "Power-Tutor" update**, featuring a full suite of professional tools: automated PDF invoicing, WhatsApp reminder integration, a smart data import wizard, and a deeply customizable gamification engine to help tutors stay motivated.

#### **The "Why":** 
I noticed that most tutoring tools were either overpriced, overly complex, or invasive with data. I built Vellor to solve this—giving "solopreneur" teachers a professional tool that runs instantly in a browser with zero setup, ensuring they own their data while enjoying a gamified experience.

#### *Tech Note: Built for speed. Every filter and sort is optimized to be $O(N)$ because even an offline tool should feel like it's running on a supercomputer.*

## 🚀 DeltaV Lab- Professional Rocket Launch Simulation 👉 [Repository Link](https://github.com/dhaatrik/professional-rocket-launch-simulation)

<div align="center">
  <img src="./public/deltav_logo.png" width="100"/>
</div>

#### **The Mission Statement:**
To bridge the gap between spaceflight games and industry-standard aerospace software by providing an accessible, open-source, and uncompromisingly accurate 4th-order physics environment for mission design and rocket simulation.

#### **Current Progress:** 
DeltaV Lab currently features a robust 50Hz deterministic physics engine using **RK4 integration**, a modular **Vehicle Assembly Building (VAB)** with real-time TWR/Delta-V calculations, and a custom **autonomous guidance DSL**. We are currently in the process of transitioning to a **"Space-Grade" hybrid architecture** (Rust/WASM) to support 6DOF dynamics, Monte-Carlo dispersion analysis, and high-fidelity environmental models like EGM2008 and NRLMSISE-00.

#### **The "Why":**
I wanted to create a platform that combines the creative freedom of Kerbal Space Program with the rigorous mathematical validation required by real-world engineering. By moving simulation logic into dedicated Web Workers and using deterministic integration, DeltaV Lab provides a high-fidelity tool for researchers, students, and "solopreneur" space enthusiasts to test complex orbital maneuvers and vehicle designs without the cost of proprietary software.

## ⛽ FuelDrop- On-Demand Fuel Delivery for the Modern World 👉 [Repository Link](https://github.com/dhaatrik/fueldrop)

<div align="center">
  <img src="./public/fueldrop_logo.png" width="100"/>
</div>

#### **The Mission Statement:**
To redefine the refueling experience by bringing the station to the user—eliminating wait times and detours through a transparent, mobile-first delivery platform designed for both individual drivers and commercial fleets.

#### **Current Progress:** 
FuelDrop is currently at **v3.0.0**, featuring a high-concurrency React 19 architecture. Key capabilities include a dual-sided interface (User & Captain dashboards), a "Fleet Mode" for bulk refueling, an "Emergency Refill" priority system with surge logic, and predictive reminders based on tank capacity and daily usage patterns.

#### **The "Why":**
Refueling is a legacy friction point in modern logistics. I built FuelDrop to solve the inefficiencies of traditional stations—specifically the lack of real-time tracking for businesses and the unnecessary time wasted by individuals. By treating fuel as a service rather than a destination, we can optimize delivery routes and reduce the carbon footprint associated with "searching for fuel".

#### 📖 **A Note on the Journey: Q1 2025 & Beyond**
FuelDrop was more than just a codebase; it was my primary startup focus during the first quarter of 2025. I’ll soon be sharing a comprehensive deep-dive article on **LinkedIn** and **Medium** detailing the entire lifecycle of this venture.

I’m a firm believer that **"knowledge kept secret is knowledge wasted"**. In this upcoming piece, I’ll be pulling back the curtain on:

- The "First-Principle" breakthroughs and the "hard-knock" lessons learned.

If you're an aspiring founder or just curious about the messiness of building from 0 to 1, stay tuned—I’m sharing it all so you can learn from my wins and, more importantly, my mistakes.

## 🤖 The Infinite Intelligence 👉 [Repository Link](https://github.com/dhaatrik/the-infinite-intelligence)
#### **The Mission Statement:**
To transcend the limitations of single-model AI by simulating a collaborative "council of experts" that debates, critiques, and synthesizes complex problems into high-fidelity, actionable intelligence.

#### **Current Progress:** 
The platform is a state-of-the-art **React 19** application featuring advanced orchestration modes like **Parallel Collaboration, Sequential Workflows,*** and **Round-Robin Debate**. It currently includes **Dynamic Agent Assembly** (Beta Mode) for tailoring expert personas to specific requests, real-time **Token Usage Tracking**, and an **Artifact Extraction** system for managing code and data in an interactive panel.

#### **The "Why":**
Standard AI outputs often suffer from single-viewpoint bias and a lack of critical self-review. I built The Infinite Intelligence to solve this "hallucination of certainty" by forcing models to decompose problems into first principles and engage in multi-round peer-review cycles before providing a final answer. It’s about moving from a single AI "assistant" to a professional-grade "council" that checks its own work.
